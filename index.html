How Day 2 works

You'll be paired with another player and rotate through three stations — putting green, chipping green, and range — in ~30-minute blocks. Your partner counts every attempt, you count theirs. Right after each station, log your makes in the My Scores tab. Your make percentages appear next to your tryout score on the leaderboard.
